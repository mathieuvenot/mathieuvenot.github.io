// L-E-G-O

 

Separation of the graphic style of this image into several "LEGO plates", smoothing deleted and selection of 8 colors.

Reassembling these plates to form a single, large and solid block.

The final block is composed of a stack of plates organized by color, evoking a gradation of lights sets, shadows and materials on a building.
