// THE DRUNKEN BOAT

 

Cover for a Batobus station.

Form and structure based on a sailboat hull. Discretization of the surface into panels, laths (strip-planking construction type), central core with water points, disabled access. Modelisation fully parametric under Grasshopper, 3D printing models (white ABS plastic).

 

Operation Site

Site located in the center of Paris, on the Quai de la Tournelle between Tournelle Bridge and the bridge of the Archdiocese. Analyzes, observations and research, especially on the traffic and river transport.

3D modeling with Marie PERRENOT and Yoona KIM
